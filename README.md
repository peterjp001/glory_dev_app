# 24hBible

24hBible is a revolutionary tool designed to help the children of God organize and follow a personalized Bible reading plan. Its goal is to make consistent Bible study simple, motivating, and spiritually enriching.

---

## 🙏 Vision

This project is created with prayer and dedication. I pray that God’s grace covers every aspect of this project, guiding it to bless and inspire all who use it in their journey of faith.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Personalized Bible Plans:** Create and follow your own reading schedule.
- **Daily Guidance:** Keep track of daily readings and progress.
- **Motivation & Discipline:** Encourage regular engagement with God’s Word.
- **Simple and Clear Design:** Easy-to-use interface to help maintain a consistent habit.

---

## 💡 Purpose

The purpose of 24hBible is not to replace the Bible but to assist children of God in building a consistent and organized Bible reading discipline, fostering spiritual growth and devotion.

---

## ❤️ Inspiration

This project is inspired by the desire to help believers develop a routine of daily Bible reading in a practical, accessible, and motivating way. May it serve as a tool to strengthen faith and knowledge of God’s Word.
